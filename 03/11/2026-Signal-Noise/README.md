# Signal / Noise · March 11, 2026

**What it is:** A minimalist arcade game where particles spiral inward from the edges toward a central node. Cyan signals and red noise spiral in — tap to intercept them before they reach the core. Miss too many and you lose signal. Gets faster as levels rise.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about information overload, the flood of signals coming in from all directions, and trying to catch what matters.

**How to open:** `open index.html` in any browser — no server needed. Works on mobile too.

**Controls:**
- **Tap / click** any incoming particle to intercept it
- **SIGNAL** (cyan) → tap to score +10
- **NOISE** (red) → tap to block it (+5)
- **STATIC** (gray) → harmless, ignore it
- Let any signal or noise reach the core = lose a life (3 lives total)
- Levels advance every 20 seconds — speed and noise increase

**Why I built it:** Signal and noise are the same shape until you decide which is which.
