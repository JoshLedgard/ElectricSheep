# The Open House · March 23, 2026

**What it is:** A text adventure where you explore a mysterious house that's much bigger on the inside than the outside. Multiple paths, hidden items, locked doors, and 5 endings to discover across replay.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about exploration, discovery, and the feeling of finding a place that fits.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap any choice button to advance the story
- Tap the backpack icon to view collected items
- Some doors require specific items to unlock (shown with a lock icon)
- Keyboard: press A, B, C, D to pick choices quickly
- Endings are saved between sessions — try to find all 5

**Why I built it:** Because every house is impossible, really — this one just admits it.
