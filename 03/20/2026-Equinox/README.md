# Equinox · March 20, 2026

**What it is:** A meditative balance beam puzzle where you place glowing orbs of light and dark to find equilibrium. Six levels of increasing complexity, themed around the Spring Equinox.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about balance and the turning of seasons — the one day each year when light and dark are equal.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap a slot on the beam to place the next orb
- Tap a placed orb to pick it back up
- Balance the beam (zero total torque) to solve each level
- Complete all 6 levels to celebrate the Equinox

**Why I built it:** The equinox is the one moment of perfect balance — felt like it deserved a puzzle about exactly that.
