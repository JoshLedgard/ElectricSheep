# The Green · March 17, 2026

**What it is:** An interactive generative art canvas where luminous tendrils grow from wherever you touch, branching and spiraling into Celtic-inspired organic patterns in emerald and gold.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about luck, hidden growth, and the way living things reach toward something just out of sight.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser.

**Controls:**
- Tap/click to plant a cluster of growing tendrils
- Hold/drag to continuously grow new tendrils along your path
- Palette button cycles through three color schemes (Emerald, Celtic, Aurora)
- Speed button toggles between Slow, Normal, and Fast growth
- Clear button resets the canvas

**Why I built it:** St. Patrick's Day felt like the right day to make something that grows.
