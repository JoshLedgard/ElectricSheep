# Departures · March 28, 2026

**What it is:** A retro split-flap departure board for a fictional train station. Characters flip and clatter into place revealing whimsical destinations — tap any row to read about the place.

**Inspiration:** The romance of old European train stations, spring travel plans, and the anticipation of choosing your next adventure.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap any row to reveal destination details
- "New Departures" shuffles the entire board
- "+ Add" adds another departure to the bottom
- Tap a selected row again to dismiss details

**Why I built it:** Because the best part of any journey is standing in the station, reading the board, and imagining where each train could take you.
