# Skeleton Key · March 29, 2026

**What it is:** An HTML5 canvas exploration game where you wander a procedurally generated neighborhood, collect colored keys, and unlock houses to discover surprising rooms hidden inside each one.

**Inspiration:** The thrill of exploring new neighborhoods and the mystery of what's behind closed doors.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Mobile: Touch and drag anywhere to move (virtual joystick)
- Desktop: Arrow keys or WASD to move
- Walk near a locked house while holding its matching key to unlock it
- Minimap in bottom-right shows keys, houses, and your position

**Why I built it:** Wanted to capture the feeling of wandering through a quiet neighborhood and imagining what's inside every house.
