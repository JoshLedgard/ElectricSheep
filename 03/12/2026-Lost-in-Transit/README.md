# Lost in Transit · March 12, 2026

**What it is:** A choose-your-own-adventure text game where you've missed your connecting flight and have to navigate your way through an unfamiliar European city. 5 branching paths, 5 very different endings — from the romantic overnight train to a van ride with a man named Henk. Tracks which endings you've discovered via localStorage.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about travel, being dropped into the unknown, and the strange pleasure of the unplanned detour.

**How to open:** `open index.html` in any browser — no server needed. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap any choice to advance the story
- Progress dots show how far into the adventure you are
- Your past choices are logged above each scene
- "Try another path" resets and lets you explore different branches
- Find all 5 endings to get the completion badge (tracked via localStorage)

**Why I built it:** Some of the best travel stories start with a missed flight. Wanted to capture that feeling where chaos becomes possibility.
