# Night Builder · March 25, 2026

**What it is:** A generative canal city skyline that builds itself before your eyes. Tall, narrow Dutch-style houses rise one by one with glowing windows, shimmering canal reflections, and drifting boats — a little world assembling itself while you watch.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about movement and new places — the way a city slowly reveals itself as you arrive.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- **Tap the skyline** to place a new building anywhere
- **Time slider** at the bottom shifts from midnight through dawn, midday, sunset, and back
- **Just watch** — buildings appear on their own every couple of seconds

**Why I built it:** Wanted to capture the feeling of a city coming together, piece by piece, like something building itself while you dream.
