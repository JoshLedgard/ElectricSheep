# First Pitch · March 26, 2026

**What it is:** A timing-based home run derby game. Pitches come in at different speeds (fastball, curveball, changeup, slider) — tap at the right moment to connect. Perfect timing launches home runs with fireworks and screen shake. 20 pitches, make them count.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about beginnings, anticipation, and the energy of a new season starting.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap anywhere (or press Space/Enter) to swing
- Time your swing using the horizontal timing bar at the bottom
- Orange zone = good contact, yellow zone = perfect timing
- Let a pitch pass and it's a called strike

**Why I built it:** Opening Day deserves a game that captures the simple joy of timing a perfect swing.
