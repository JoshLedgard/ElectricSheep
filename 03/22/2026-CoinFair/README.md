# Is This Coin Fair? · March 22, 2026

**What it is:** A Bayesian coin-flip explorer — flip a mystery coin and watch a live probability distribution sharpen in real time as evidence accumulates, revealing whether the coin is fair or biased.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about evidence, probability, and how long it takes to actually be sure of something.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap **Flip** (or press Space) to flip the coin
- Watch the belief distribution and confidence meter update live
- After 30+ flips, tap **Reveal Truth** to see the coin's hidden bias
- Tap **New Coin** to start a fresh experiment

**Why I built it:** Because the gap between "I think I know" and "I actually know" is wider than most people realize, and watching a Beta distribution prove it is deeply satisfying.
