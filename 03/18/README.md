# Check In · March 18, 2026

**What it is:** A hotel lobby speed game where guests arrive with specific needs and you match them to the best available room before they lose patience. Perfect matches build combos, wrong rooms cost points, and the pace ramps up as your shift goes on.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about logistics, empty rooms, and the art of matching the right thing to the right person under pressure.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap/click a room to assign the current guest
- Match guest need tags (WiFi, View, Quiet, etc.) to room traits
- Perfect matches (all needs met) earn big points + combo multiplier
- Bonus points for matching the guest's priority trait
- 5 misses (bad matches or walkouts) ends your shift
- Personal best saved automatically

**Why I built it:** Wanted to turn the feeling of triaging incoming requests into a satisfying action game — scanning, matching, deciding fast.
