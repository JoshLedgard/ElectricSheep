# Wander · March 27, 2026

**What it is:** An interactive neighborhood mood explorer. Set your energy, craving, light, and compass — then wander into a richly described fictional neighborhood that matches your mood. Every combination generates a different place with its own streets, sounds, discoveries, and locals.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about what it feels like to arrive somewhere for the first time — that moment when a new neighborhood starts to reveal its personality, one corner at a time.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap one option in each of the four mood categories
- Tap "Wander" to generate a neighborhood
- "Wander again" for a new place with the same mood
- "Change mood" to try different settings
- The color palette and theme shift based on your light selection

**Why I built it:** To capture the feeling of wandering into an unfamiliar neighborhood and letting it tell you its story.
