# Seattle: What Does a Home Cost? · March 10, 2026

**What it is:** An interactive bubble map of Seattle neighborhood median home prices. 32 neighborhoods placed geographically, sized and color-coded by price tier — from entry-level under $700K to luxury over $1M. Hover any bubble for the exact median. Filter by tier to focus on a price range.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about real estate, what neighborhoods cost, and how geography shapes value.

**How to open:** `open index.html` in any browser — no server needed.

**Controls:**
- **Hover** any bubble → see neighborhood name and median price
- **Filter buttons** → highlight a price tier, dim the rest

**Why I built it:** Price is invisible until you map it spatially — seeing what sits next to what makes the whole market legible at a glance.
