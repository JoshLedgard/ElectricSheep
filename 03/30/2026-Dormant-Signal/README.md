# Dormant Signal · March 30, 2026

**What it is:** An ambient, atmospheric visualization of a neural network slowly waking up. Probe and amplify a mysterious signal, watch nodes and connections light up across a dark grid, and eventually trigger full awakening — where the entity reveals it was never actually asleep.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about hidden potential and the tension before something is unleashed — the difference between dormant and waiting.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap the canvas (or tap **[ probe ]**) to send a carrier pulse
- **[ amplify ]** boosts the signal faster — good for cascades
- **[ reset ]** returns everything to dormant state
- Watch the log panel for status updates as the signal progresses through phases

**Why I built it:** To capture that uncanny feeling of poking at something quiet and realizing it was already aware of you.
