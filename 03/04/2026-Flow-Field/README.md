# Flow Field · March 4, 2026

**What it is:** 3,500 particles navigating a Perlin noise flow field, leaving color trails as they move. Hypnotic, generative, different every time.

**How to open:** `open ~/daily-surprise/2026-03-04/index.html` — runs in any browser, no server needed.

**Controls:**
- **Click** anywhere to burst new particles at that spot
- **Space** to regenerate with a new random seed
- **C** to cycle through 5 color palettes (ocean, fire, aurora, gold, electric)
- **T** to toggle trail decay speed

**Why I built it:** Flow fields are one of the most beautiful things you can make with noise functions — I wanted to build something you'd actually leave open on a monitor just to watch.
