# Pathways · March 21, 2026

**What it is:** A generative art piece where luminous trails bloom from your touch, curving through a noise field toward glowing waypoints. Trails gently seek out destinations and bloom with light when they arrive.

**Inspiration:** The beauty of trajectories — many possible paths from a single starting point, each finding its own way.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap anywhere to launch a burst of trails
- Drag to scatter trails continuously
- Palette button cycles through 4 color themes (Aurora, Ember, Ocean, Dusk)
- Clear button resets the canvas and generates new waypoints
- Waypoints button toggles the destination points on/off

**Why I built it:** To make something meditative about the gap between where you are and where you could go.
