# Fresh Page · March 31, 2026

**What it is:** An interactive journal page for the last day of March. Write a closing thought, then turn the page with a satisfying 3D flip to reveal a pristine April 1 — complete with confetti, sparkles, and a wax seal to make it official.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about endings and new beginnings — that satisfying moment of turning to a blank page.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Type in the writing area to jot your thoughts
- Tap "Turn the page" (or swipe left) to flip to April
- Tap "Back to March" (or swipe right) to flip back
- Write something on the April page to reveal the wax seal
- Tap the seal to stamp it and make your page official
- Arrow keys also work for turning pages

**Why I built it:** The last day of a quarter deserves a moment — a pause between what was and what's next.
