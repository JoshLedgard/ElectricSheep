# Drift · March 16, 2026

**What it is:** A minimalist ice-sliding puzzle. Your piece glides in one direction until it hits a wall — tap arrows or swipe to navigate to the glowing star. 10 levels, each with a par score to beat. Move counter + personal best tracker.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about momentum, finding your way through friction, and the satisfying click of things sliding into place.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:** Arrow keys / WASD on desktop · Tap the arrow buttons or swipe on mobile · Press `r` to restart a level

**Why I built it:** Ice-sliding puzzles are deceptively simple — one rule, tons of depth — and they're perfect for a quick satisfying play on your phone.
