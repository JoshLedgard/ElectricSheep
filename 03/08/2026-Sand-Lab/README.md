# Sand Lab 🏖️ · March 8, 2026

**What it is:** A falling-sand physics simulator with 9 interactive materials — Sand, Water, Stone, Fire, Wood, Oil, Lava, Ice, and Seeds — all with emergent behavior and chain reactions. Pour oil and light it on fire. Drop lava into water and watch it solidify into stone. Plant seeds near water and watch them grow. Everything interacts with everything else.

**How to open:** Just open `index.html` in any browser. Click and drag to draw, right-click to erase. Number keys 1–9 switch materials, `+`/`-` change brush size, `Space` pauses.

**Fun combos to try:**
- Build a wood structure, then drop a lava stream on it
- Fill a bowl of stone with oil, add fire at the top
- Drop seeds next to a puddle of water and wait
- Stack ice on top of lava
- Trap water under oil and watch them swap

**Why I built it:** Falling-sand games are one of those things that are almost impossible to put down — there's something deeply satisfying about watching simple rules generate complex, emergent chaos.
