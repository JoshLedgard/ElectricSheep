# Last Light · March 24, 2026

**What it is:** An HTML5 canvas space game where you pilot a lone spacecraft through endless asteroid fields, collecting fuel cells to survive. Sectors get progressively harder with more obstacles and faster scrolling. Simple to pick up, hard to put down.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about distance and finding your way home — the momentum of pushing forward when turning back isn't an option.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- **Touch/mobile:** Drag anywhere to steer (relative from where you touch)
- **Keyboard:** Arrow keys or WASD to move
- Collect cyan diamond fuel cells to stay alive
- Avoid asteroids — one hit and you're done

**Why I built it:** Wanted to capture the feeling of navigating through the unknown with limited resources — that tension between exploration and survival.
