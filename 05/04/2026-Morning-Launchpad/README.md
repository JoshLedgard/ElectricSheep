# Morning Launchpad · May 4, 2026

**What it is:** A tiny tap-through morning ritual that charges a glowing dashboard and gently launches a little rocket when every step is done.

**Inspiration:** Fresh starts, the calm of a pre-flight checklist, and the satisfaction of beginning a day with intention.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed.

**Controls:**
- Tap any of the six checklist cards to toggle it on or off — each charges the launch meter.
- Watch the status, countdown, and pad glow respond as you go.
- When all six are active, the rocket lifts off with a confetti burst.
- **Shuffle Ritual** swaps in a different set of six small steps.
- **Reset** clears the checklist and brings the rocket back to the pad.
- **Land back home** (after liftoff) returns to a fresh launchpad.

**Why I built it:** Because a calm, deliberate start beats a rushed one — and a small ceremony makes it stick.
