# Compass of Tiny Currents · May 6, 2026

**What it is:** A pocket-sized, one-thumb text adventure. Steer a paper boat through a small archipelago, make tiny choices at each island, and gather currents that string themselves into a private constellation at the lighthouse.

**Inspiration:** Small systems finding their quiet way back into motion.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server, installs, or network needed.

**Controls:** Tap the gold "Cast off" button to begin. At each island, tap one of three choice cards to gather a current. The boat moves on its own; the meter at the top tracks your four currents (Clarity, Spark, Rest, Momentum). After five islands you reach the lighthouse — tap "Cast off again" to replay with a freshly shuffled order.

**Why I built it:** A calm, finished two-or-three-minute interlude where every path leads home and the only ask is which current you'd like more of today.
