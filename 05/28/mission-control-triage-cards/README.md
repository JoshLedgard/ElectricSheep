# Mission Control Triage Cards

**Date:** 2026-05-28  
**Format choice:** Practical workflow/rubric micro-tool — intentionally different from the last three pocket ritual toys (lantern atlas, rainbell foundry, compass loom). This one is a small decision aid rather than a poetic generator or field-note card.

## What it is

Mission Control Triage Cards is a tiny mobile-first prioritization board. Load a few work cards — Tiny Win, Customer Ask, Risky Change, Research Bet, Bug Fix, or a custom card — then tune each card on three simple axes:

- **Impact**: how much it matters if it lands.
- **Certainty**: how confident the next step is.
- **Friction**: how much drag or coordination it carries.

Tap **Sort the queue** and the page routes cards into a compact action plan: **Do Now**, **Delegate / Automate**, **Probe**, or **Park**. Each card includes a score and a short explanation so the result feels useful, not just decorative.

## Privacy-safe inspiration

The inspiration is generic and thematic: recent emphasis on reliable workflows, quick audits, small automation improvements, and making the Daily Surprise feel like a dreamed Scoob tool for Josh instead of another generic poetic toy.

No private chat quotes, sensitive details, names, emails, addresses, credentials, or financial information are included.

## How to open

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. It runs entirely in the page with no server, package install, external assets, or network calls.

## Controls

- Tap preset cards to add or remove them from the queue.
- Add a custom card with the input field.
- Use the Impact, Certainty, and Friction sliders to tune each card.
- Tap **Sort the queue** to generate the action plan.
- Tap **Reset sample** to restore the starter deck.

## Why it was built

This is a small prototype of the kind of lightweight decision board Scoob could build around recurring work: quick enough to use in a spare minute, structured enough to turn a messy queue into a next action, and playful enough to still feel like a Daily Surprise.
