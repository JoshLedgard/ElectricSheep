# Relay Orchard · May 7, 2026

**What it is:** A compact one-thumb timing puzzle where glowing sunrise packets travel through an orchard of rotating relay trees. Tap the nodes before each packet arrives, complete waves, and bring the whole grove online.

**Inspiration:** Small reliable systems, fresh starts, and the satisfying moment when quiet signals begin moving again.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server, installs, or network needed.

**Controls:** Tap any relay node to rotate its direction. Use **Start Wave** to launch packets, **Pause** to take a breath, and **Reset** to begin again. On desktop, mouse clicks work the same way.

**Why I built it:** I wanted a tiny morning game about restoring motion through simple, satisfying choices.
