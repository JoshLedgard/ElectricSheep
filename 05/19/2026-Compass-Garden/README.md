# Compass Garden — May 19, 2026

## What it is
A pocket compass-dial garden. Tap inside the calm compass face to plant glowing seed-stars in five colors, switch between night / dawn / meadow skies, nudge or clear the layout, and then "Compose Route" to mint a small named garden route with a tiny motto generated from the pattern you made.

It's a 2–3 minute, mobile-first interactive toy. Up to twelve seed-stars per garden. The composed name, motto, and "garden number" are deterministic — the same pattern always produces the same little route — but the shape, bearing, and count of your seeds all influence the result, so even small nudges give a new feel.

## Privacy-safe inspiration
Built around generic themes only: fresh start, morning curiosity, playful systems, calm beginnings, gentle order, small adventures. No real names, numbers, addresses, conversation details, or personal references.

## How to open
- Tap `index.html` (works in any browser, no install, no network).
- Best on a phone or tablet held in portrait.

## Controls
- **Seed row (top):** pick a seed-star color — Lantern, Rose, Mint, Sky, or Violet.
- **Dial:** tap anywhere inside the compass to plant a seed-star. Each new seed numbers in plant order and is connected by a soft dashed thread.
- **Sky row:** choose Night, Dawn, or Meadow to retint the dial.
- **Nudge:** if the dial is empty, sprinkles five starter seeds; otherwise jiggles every existing seed slightly.
- **Clear:** removes all seeds and the result card.
- **Compose Route** (unlocks at 3+ seeds): generates a named route, a one-line description, and a tiny motto sentence.

## Why it was built
A small daily surprise — one self-contained creative-coding toy per day. This one leans into the feeling of a calm, slow-morning planning ritual: lay down a few small intentions in a circle, see the shape, give it a name and a sentence to walk into the day with.

Self-contained, runs offline, no dependencies.
