# Launch Ritual Lab · May 1, 2026

**What it is:** A mobile-first single-page tool for turning a tiny project into something ready to release. Adjust scope, spark, and calm; complete a quick checklist; then generate a playful launch card.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about reliable handoffs, finishing rituals, and the satisfying moment when something is truly ready.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed.

**Controls:**
- Drag the **Scope**, **Spark**, and **Calm** sliders
- Tap checklist items to mark pre-flight steps complete
- Tap **Launch** to animate the rocket and create a launch card
- Tap **Reset** to start over

**Why I built it:** I wanted a practical little ritual that makes “ready enough to ship” feel calm, visible, and fun.
