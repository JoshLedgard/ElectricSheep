# Signal Garden · May 1, 2026

**What it is:** A calm mobile-first generative toy. Tap an empty field to plant glowing "signals" that grow into bioluminescent flowers. As neighbors mature they reach toward each other and exchange tiny handoffs along curved threads. Three handoffs and a plant turns *ready* — gold halo, soft pollen burst.

**Inspiration:** The quiet morning satisfaction of watching little system signals come up green one by one — readiness as something to grow, not just to wait for.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed.

**Controls:**
- **Tap the field** — plant a signal at that spot
- **Plant** — drop a signal somewhere random
- **Breeze** — send a gust; flowers bend, pollen scatters
- **Day / Night** — toggle between phosphorescent night and warm cream daylight
- **Reset** — clear the field

**Why I built it:** I wanted a two-minute morning ritual — something that turns "is everything ready?" into a small living picture instead of a checklist. Plant a few. Watch them link up. Sweep a breeze. Notice when the field goes quiet and golden.
