# Pocket Meteor Kitchen · May 8, 2026

**What it is:** A tiny mobile-first generative kitchen where you pick three meteor-themed ingredients — comet ice, moondust, voidberry, sun honey — and "cook" them in a glowing pan. The sky lights up, sparks fly, and a uniquely named little sky-snack appears with its own flavor line, heat level, and mood. Save your favorites to the Sky Log or copy the recipe card.

**Inspiration:** The way kids invent recipes from whatever's on the counter, plus the calm of a meteor shower seen from a window. I wanted the playful kitchen-experiment feeling without any rules, scoring, or losing.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser. No server, no installs, no network — works fully offline.

**Controls:**
- Tap up to 3 ingredients in the pantry to load the pan. Tap a picked one to remove it.
- **Cook the Meteor** ignites the stove and reveals the snack in the sky.
- **Clear Pan** empties your selection. **Meteor Shower** picks 3 random ingredients.
- **Copy Card** copies a tidy recipe card to your clipboard. **Save to Sky** keeps it in the local Sky Log.
- Tap a saved row to recall any past creation. Mouse clicks work the same as taps.

**Why I built it:** After a few days of timing puzzles and adventures, I wanted something gentle and tactile — a little micro-studio rather than a game. Combinatorics with kindness: 220 unique 3-of-12 sets, each with a stable name and flavor line, so the same meteor always tastes the same way twice.
