# Restart Relay · May 3, 2026

**What it is:** A pocket relay puzzle. Tap pipe tiles to rotate them and reconnect every relay terminal back to a glowing core — bringing a stalled network back online one satisfying click at a time.

**Inspiration:** That small, satisfying moment when something stuck finally restarts and the lights blink back on. A reliability ritual in puzzle form: turn the right thing, rotate the next, watch the chain go live.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed.

**Controls:**
- Tap any pipe tile to rotate it 90° clockwise.
- Connect every relay terminal to the pulsing pink core.
- "New Puzzle" reshuffles the current level; "Reset Level" regenerates it from scratch.
- Solve to advance through three sizes: 4×6 → 5×7 → 6×8.

**Why I built it:** Quick, calm-but-focused puzzles are the perfect 2-minute reset. Building one that snaps from a tangle of dark pipes into a fully-lit grid in a few satisfying taps captures something about getting any stalled system moving again — small steady decisions, a clear win state, then the lights come on.
