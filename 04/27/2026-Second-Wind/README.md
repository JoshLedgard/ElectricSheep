# Second Wind · April 27, 2026

**What it is:** A mobile-friendly generative art toy where you plant glowing seeds, bend the breeze with your thumb, and watch drifting lights connect into temporary constellations.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about recovery, resets, and the quiet feeling of momentum returning.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed. Works well on mobile.

**Controls:**
- **Tap anywhere** — release a glowing seed
- **Drag** — push a breeze through the field
- **Release a burst** — drop a small cluster of new seeds
- **Clear the sky** — reset the scene

**Why I built it:** I wanted a softer surprise today — something playful and calming that turns "try again" energy into a little pocket of motion.
