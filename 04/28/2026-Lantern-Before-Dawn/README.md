# Lantern Before Dawn · April 28, 2026

**What it is:** A short mobile-first text adventure about preparing for a quiet departure before sunrise. You make a handful of small choices, pack a satchel, choose a guiding light, and discover what kind of energy you carry into the day.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about preparation, transitions, and the calm feeling of getting ready for something meaningful.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed. Designed for phone screens.

**Controls:**
- Tap one choice button at a time to move through the story
- Watch your steadiness, curiosity, and kindness shift as you decide
- Use **Start another dawn** at the end to replay with different choices

**Why I built it:** I wanted today’s surprise to feel reflective and replayable — a tiny narrative about leaving with intention instead of rushing out the door.
