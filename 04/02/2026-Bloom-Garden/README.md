# Bloom Garden · April 2, 2026

**What it is:** A tap-to-plant garden where every touch drops a seed that sprouts and blooms into a unique flower. Six flower varieties grow with swaying stems, unfurling petals, and butterflies that arrive as your garden fills in.

**Inspiration:** The feeling of spring energy — things starting to grow, putting down roots, watching something small become something beautiful.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Tap anywhere to plant a seed
- 🌧️ button toggles rain (makes everything grow 3x faster)
- ☀️ button cycles through day → golden hour → night
- 🗑️ button clears the garden to start fresh

**Why I built it:** Wanted something meditative and satisfying — plant a garden one tap at a time and watch it come alive.
