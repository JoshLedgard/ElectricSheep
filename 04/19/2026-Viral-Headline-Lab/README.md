# Viral Headline Lab · April 19, 2026

**What it is:** A playful headline scoring tool. Type any headline, tweet, or slogan and it scores it 0–100 for "virality" with a breakdown across curiosity, emotion, urgency, specificity, and punch — plus a one-liner roast. Tap Surprise Me to generate clickbait-style headlines from a template engine, then remix or save your favorites to a personal hit list.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about the slightly absurd science of what makes things spread online — the patterns, power words, and tiny tricks that make a headline impossible to scroll past.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser. No server, no install. Saves your hit list to localStorage.

**Controls:**
- **✨ Surprise Me** — generate a fresh headline
- **Score It** — score whatever's in the box
- **🔀 Remix** — toss the current one and try another
- **⭐ Save** — pin a banger to your hit list
- **📋 Copy** — copy the headline to clipboard
- Tap a saved item to load it back into the lab

**Why I built it:** A small toy about the meta side of creative work — a tool that's about tools, a headline about headlines, a little wink at how much of marketing is just word-shaped pattern matching.
