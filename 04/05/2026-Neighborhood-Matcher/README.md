# Neighborhood Matcher · April 5, 2026

**What it is:** An interactive priority matcher — dial in what matters to you (schools, space, walkability, nature, vibe, commute, food/nightlife) and get a ranked list of 12 fictional neighborhoods with match scores, bar breakdowns, and personality blurbs. Every slider adjustment reshapes the results instantly.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about place, priorities, and what makes somewhere feel like home.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser. No server needed.

**Controls:** Drag the sliders to weight each factor (0 = doesn't matter, 10 = essential). Tap "Find My Match" to rank neighborhoods. Tap "Recalculate" to update after adjusting.

**Why I built it:** Wanted to explore how small changes in what you value completely flip which place wins — same data, totally different answers depending on the person.
