# Pack Your Bag · April 4, 2026

**What it is:** A mystery packing game — choose 8 items from 20, hit "Zip it up", and your destination is revealed. Your score shows how well your choices matched: great calls, useful extras, dead weight, and what you wish you'd grabbed. Six destinations, fully random.

**Inspiration:** The thrill of going somewhere, the ritual of packing, the little decisions that reveal what kind of traveler you are.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:** Tap any item to select/deselect it. You get exactly 8 slots. Tap "Zip it up" to see where you're going and how well you packed for it.

**Why I built it:** Packing is a tiny game of prediction — you're betting on a future you don't fully know yet. That felt worth turning into something you can play.
