# Drift · April 21, 2026

**What it is:** A meditative interactive network — glowing nodes drifting through darkness, connected by threads of light that fade as energy drains. Tap or click anywhere to send a pulse of energy rippling through nearby nodes, briefly lighting up the web.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about silence and signals — what it feels like when a network goes quiet, and the small act of reaching out to wake something back up.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed.

**Controls:** Tap or click anywhere to send an energy pulse to nearby nodes. Watch the connections brighten and fade. Try pulsing the same area repeatedly.

**Why I built it:** Wanted something calm and tactile — an ambient toy where your touch has a visible, spreading effect.
