# Safe Harbor · April 3, 2026

**What it is:** A lighthouse keeper game — sweep your beam across dark waters to illuminate lost ships and guide them safely to harbor before they crash on the rocks. Ships arrive in waves, getting faster and more numerous.

**Inspiration:** The quiet act of looking out for others, welcoming what's coming your way, and keeping up when things speed up — spring nights on the coast.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Drag anywhere to aim the lighthouse beam
- Tap/hold to point the beam where ships need light
- Ships glow cyan when illuminated and steer toward harbor
- Unlit ships flash red SOS when close to the rocks
- Lose a life when a ship crashes or drifts off-screen
- Waves get faster — keep up!

**Why I built it:** Sometimes being there with a light is all it takes.
