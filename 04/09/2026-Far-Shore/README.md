# Far Shore · April 9, 2026

**What it is:** A canvas exploration game where you sail a small vessel across uncharted, fog-covered waters, discovering hidden islands. Each voyage generates a new map — find all seven islands to complete your journey.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about journeys and discovery — setting off with no map and seeing what you find.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser

**Controls:** Tap or click anywhere to set your heading. Your boat sails toward where you point. A compass in the corner hints at the nearest undiscovered island. Edge arrows point to off-screen islands.

**Why I built it:** Wanted to capture the feeling of sailing into fog and the thrill of spotting land.
