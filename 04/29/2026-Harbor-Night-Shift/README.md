# Harbor Night Shift · April 29, 2026

**What it is:** A compact mobile-friendly harbor routing game. Tap incoming boats, match them to the right docks, and keep the water from getting too crowded as the pace rises.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about arrivals, timing, and the calm skill of making room for the right things.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed.

**Controls:**
- Tap a boat to select it
- Tap the matching dock symbol to settle it
- Use **Start shift** to begin and **Reset harbor** to start over
- If pressure reaches 100%, tap anywhere to replay

**Why I built it:** I wanted a playful little coordination game that turns scheduling and incoming requests into something light, tidy, and satisfying.
