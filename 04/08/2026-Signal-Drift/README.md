# Signal Drift · April 8, 2026

**What it is:** A generative art piece where luminous particles drift through a noise field, leaving glowing trails as they flow between pulsing waypoints. Streams emerge, travel, and fade — an ever-changing canvas of light in motion.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about journeys and systems in motion — signals departing into the unknown.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser

**Controls:** Tap or click anywhere to launch a burst of new signals. Drag to scatter particles along your path.

**Why I built it:** Wanted something hypnotic and calming — just light drifting through space, endlessly.
