# Nothing Works · April 1, 2026

**What it is:** A perfectly normal settings page where every toggle, slider, and button does something hilariously wrong. Dark mode flips the page upside down, volume cranks out confetti, privacy mode adds googly eyes. Find all 12 hidden pranks.

**Inspiration:** The universal experience of trusting a settings page — and the one day a year you shouldn't.

**How to open:** Open index.html in any browser. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- Toggle switches, sliders, buttons, and dropdowns — tap everything
- Progress bar tracks how many pranks you've discovered
- Tap the "Premium" badge 3 times for a secret
- Find all 12 pranks to unlock the finale

**Why I built it:** Because on April Fools Day, nothing should work the way you expect it to.
