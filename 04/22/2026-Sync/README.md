# Sync · April 22, 2026

**What it is:** A fast-paced grid game where you race to bring 36 nodes back online before time runs out. Tap cells to sync them — matching colors chain automatically. Build combos for score multipliers, and use your two Cascade charges to trigger explosive cluster syncs.

**Inspiration:** Thinking about systems coming back online, catching up after a gap, and the satisfying feeling of everything clicking back into place.

**How to open:** Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed. Works great on mobile.

**Controls:**
- **Tap a cell** — sync it; same-color neighbors auto-chain
- **⚡ Cascade** — triggers a random cluster sync (2 charges)
- **↺ Reset** — start over with a fresh grid
- Tap fast for combo multipliers — combos reset after 2s of inactivity

**Why I built it:** Wanted something quick and satisfying — the kind of game you can play in 90 seconds and feel a tiny hit of accomplishment.
